The AI Collider

The AI Collider profiles AI stances — corporate strategies, regulatory frameworks, technical architectures — across dozens of dimensions as colour-coded grids, where each cell is a position and each colour is the principle that drives it. Select stances to compare them side by side: matching colours show shared logic; contrasts reveal where positions genuinely diverge; gaps show what each stance leaves unsaid.
